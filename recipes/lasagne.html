Delicious Lasagna in one hour

This is a simple recipe for delicious lasagne. Prep time is about 20 min. Cooking time will be 40 min. The ingredients is as follows:

Ingredients:

  • 400g beef
  • Lasagna plates
  • Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook beef
  2. Mix in vegetables and spices
  3. Let simmer for about 15min or more for more flavor
  4. Make layers of meat sauce and lasagna plates
  5. Top with Cheese
