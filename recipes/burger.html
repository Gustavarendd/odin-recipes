Burgers

Simple delicious burgers

Follow this recipe and you will have delicious burgers in no time at all.

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Different sauces after taste

Steps:

  1. Cook meat, medium rare (2min on each side)
  2. Heat buns, in preheated oven at 200 degrees, for 5min
  3. Cut Lettuce and tomatoes into desired size
  4. Serve with different sauces
